08 - Phase 7: Postcheck + Autopilot

Goal
Close run cleanly, measure deltas, and keep future automation in motion.

What the phase does
- Performs post-run snapshot/report workflow.
- Persists run/action outcomes for audit and rollback visibility.
- Leaves scheduled/autopilot artifacts for ongoing cadence.

Key benefit
- Every run has traceability.
- Team can review exactly what happened and what changed.

Operator controls
- Run monitor and action logs.
- Rollback actions where supported.
- Pause/adjust future automation through client settings and policies.
