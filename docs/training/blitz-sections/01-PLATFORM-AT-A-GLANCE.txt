01 - Platform At A Glance

What Blitz does for us
Blitz is an agency execution platform that runs Google Business Profile optimization workflows at scale.
It does not only recommend changes. It runs approved actions, tracks them, and logs outcomes.

What is automated right now
- GBP OAuth connect and client/location seeding into your org.
- Blitz run orchestration across all major phases.
- Content generation and posting pipeline with TinyURL + QR-on-image logic.
- Media queueing and upload workflows.
- Review reply automation against live GBP reviews.
- Review request dispatch queue (SMS/email) using Twilio/SendGrid.
- Policy controls, action logs, and Actions Needed escalation workflow.
- Attribution connectors and reporting surfaces for GBP + GA4 + Ads.

What operators still control
- Which clients are active in platform.
- Tone/objectives/system instructions per client.
- Photo buckets and allowed assets.
- Approval for risky actions via Actions Needed queue.
- Manual overrides for sensitive review cases.

Main team value
- Faster execution across many clients.
- Consistent SOP and audit trails.
- Better velocity on freshness/reviews/content signals.
