TRD Blitz AI - Blitz Sections Training Pack

Purpose
This folder is the team training curriculum for Blitz Agent operations.
Use it to train account managers, operators, and analysts on exactly what each Blitz section does.

9) 09-CLIENT-WORKSPACE-TAB-GUIDE.txt
10) 10-REVIEW-MESSAGING-TWILIO-A2P-GUIDE.txt

How to use in team training
- Read one section per module.
- Demo the matching page in the app immediately after each section.
- End each module by assigning one operator task and one analyst KPI review task.
