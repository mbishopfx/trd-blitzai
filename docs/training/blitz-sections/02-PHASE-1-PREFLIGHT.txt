02 - Phase 1: Preflight

Goal
Validate environment before any profile-changing action is executed.

What the phase does
- Checks integration health and token freshness.
- Validates policy constraints and action caps.
- Verifies run readiness for downstream phases.

Why it matters
- Prevents failed runs caused by expired auth or quota conditions.
- Reduces unsafe or out-of-policy action attempts.

Operator checklist
- Confirm client has active GBP integration.
- Confirm policy settings are configured.
- Confirm no open blockers in Actions Needed.

Success signal
Run proceeds into completeness/media/content phases without hard-stop errors.
