1) WHAT THIS PLATFORM IS
Blitz AI Agent is an operator-controlled autonomous execution platform for Google Business Profile growth.
It does not stop at recommendations. It executes approved actions through policy gates, logs every action,
and tracks impact across GBP metrics, GA4 data, and Google Ads spend.

In plain terms:
- Most tools tell you what to do.
- Blitz AI Agent does the work at scale with guardrails.

2) THE CORE BUSINESS OUTCOME
The platform is designed to create rapid local visibility momentum by coordinating:
- Profile completeness improvements
- Fresh media and posting activity
- Faster review response velocity
- Interaction-driving optimization
- Measurable attribution and reporting

The value proposition to clients:
"We can move your local visibility faster because we run high-quality optimization workflows continuously,
not manually once in a while."

3) WHY IT MATTERS IN 2026
Local search outcomes now depend heavily on:
- Structured profile quality
- Freshness and consistency of activity
- Engagement behaviors (calls, directions, clicks)
- Trust and review quality
- AI-readable factual coverage

Blitz AI Agent aligns operations directly with those signals.

4) HOW THE PLATFORM IS STRUCTURED
User-facing control plane (Vercel):
- Organization and client onboarding
- Integration setup (GBP, GA4, Google Ads)
- Blitz run launch + monitoring + rollback
- Policy management
- Attribution and billing snapshots

Execution + data services:
- Queue-driven autonomous workflows
- Policy checks and risk handling
- Audit-safe action logging
- Connection and token lifecycle handling

5) WHAT "AUTONOMOUS" MEANS HERE
Autonomous does not mean uncontrolled.
Autonomous means:
- The platform executes defined workflows without waiting on repetitive manual work.
- Every action still follows policy rules and role boundaries.
- Actions are visible, traceable, and reversible where supported.

6) BLITZ PROTOCOL MODULES (CURRENT UI MODEL)
The launch flow supports protocol module toggles that are attached to run policy snapshots:
- Instant Completeness Overhaul
- Visual and Media Flood
- GEO Content Barrage
- Review and Response Ignition
- Interaction Velocity Booster
- Competitor Benchmarking
- Continuous Autopilot

Practical meaning:
- We can run full Blitz mode for aggressive optimization.
- We can narrow modules for compliance-sensitive or low-bandwidth client scenarios.

7) COMPLIANCE POSITIONING FOR TEAM USE
The platform must be operated with these non-negotiables:
- No fake reviews or synthetic engagement tactics.
- No deceptive keyword stuffing.
- No policy-violating content automation.
- No hidden client actions without traceability.

All team members should explain autonomy as:
"High-speed execution with policy controls and full audit visibility."

8) HOW WE EXPLAIN IT TO CLIENTS
Simple script:
"We use an AI operations platform that continuously improves your Google Business Profile by executing
approved optimization actions, monitoring performance, and adapting weekly. You get faster movement,
faster response times, and clearer attribution."

9) SUCCESS METRICS WE TRACK
Primary:
- Profile views
- Calls
- Directions
- Website clicks
- Review response coverage and speed
- Conversion lift and blended cost per result

Secondary:
- Action completion rate
- Run success rate
- Recovery speed on failures
- Policy exception rate

10) INTERNAL EXPECTATION
This is not a passive dashboard product.
This is an agency operations engine.
If a team member is not checking runs, policies, attribution, and timeline signals weekly,
we are underutilizing the system.
